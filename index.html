About Igbajo Baptist Medical Center

The history of Igbajo Baptist Medical Center goes back 2014. It was wholly sponsored by the Igbajo Baptist Community.

The hospital which started well with firm support of the larger community had qualified health workers...later faced with some challenges that necessitated upgrade oand restructuring.

Hence, the clarion call for the the new hospital.

The new hospital is expected to serve Igbajo community and nearby towns and villages.
